---

## 🎯 How to Use

### 1. Upload Images (Optional)
- Click camera icon for Breakfast
- Click camera icon for Lunch
- Click camera icon for Dinner

### 2. Type Food (Optional)
- Type in text boxes what you ate
- Example: "2 idlis, 1 bowl sambar, 1 glass milk"

### 3. Analyze
- Click **"🔍 Analyze My Food Intake"** button

### 4. View Results
- Detected Foods (what was found)
- Nutrition Summary (calories, protein, iron, calcium)
- Recommendations (what to eat next)
