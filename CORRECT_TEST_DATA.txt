┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ UNDERSTANDING THE MODEL                                                      │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

NORMAL RISK requires:
✅ Very low blood pressure (< 105/65)
✅ Low blood sugar (< 6.2)
✅ Normal temperature (< 98.2)
✅ Low heart rate (< 72)
✅ Younger age (< 22)

MEDIUM RISK includes:
• Slightly elevated BP (105-135)
• Moderate blood sugar (6.2-9.0)
• Normal to slightly elevated vitals
• Most pregnancy cases fall here

HIGH RISK includes:
• High BP (> 135)
• High blood sugar (> 9.0)
• Elevated heart rate (> 90)
• Multiple risk factors
