👶 Your Baby's Feeding Guide

Age-appropriate nutrition for healthy growth

0-6 Months
🍼

Exclusive Breastfeeding

  • Only breast milk needed
  • No water or solid food
  • Feed every 2-3 hours
  • 6-8 feeds per day
  • Follow baby's hunger cues
6-12 Months
🥣

Introducing Solids

  • Start with single-ingredient foods
  • Ragi porridge (iron-rich)
  • Mashed banana
  • Rice + dal mash
  • Vegetable puree
  • Continue breastfeeding
1-2 Years
🍽️

Balanced Toddler Diet

  • Milk (2-3 cups daily)
  • Egg (1 per day)
  • Rice & soft vegetables
  • Fresh seasonal fruits
  • Small, frequent meals
  • Introduce family foods
